üìö Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with TherapyConnect?

+
Getting started is easy! First, create a free account by clicking "Sign Up" and completing your profile. Then, browse our network of licensed therapists, filtering by specialty, availability, and session type. Once you find a therapist you like, book your first session. You can start with a 7-day free trial to explore our features.

What types of therapy are available?

+
We offer individual therapy, couples therapy, family therapy, and group sessions. Our therapists specialize in various approaches including CBT, DBT, psychodynamic therapy, EMDR, mindfulness-based therapy, and more. Sessions are available via video, phone, or secure messaging.

Are the therapists licensed?

+
Yes! All therapists on TherapyConnect are licensed mental health professionals (LCSWs, LMFTs, LPCs, PhDs, PsyDs) who have been thoroughly vetted. They must maintain active licenses, carry malpractice insurance, and undergo background checks. You can view each therapist's credentials and specialties on their profile.

How do I book a therapy session?

+
Navigate to the "Find a Therapist" page, browse or search for a therapist, then click "Book Session" on their profile. Choose your preferred date and time from their availability, select the session type (video, phone, or in-person if available), and complete the booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with session details and a calendar invite.

Can I cancel or reschedule a session?

+
Yes! You can cancel or reschedule sessions at least 24 hours in advance for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the session are charged 50%. No-shows are charged the full session fee. To cancel, go to "My Sessions" and click "Cancel" or "Reschedule" on the appointment.

How long are therapy sessions?

+
Standard individual therapy sessions are 50-60 minutes. Initial consultations may be 30 minutes. Couples and family sessions are typically 60-90 minutes. You can see the session duration when booking.

How much does therapy cost?

+
Session fees vary by therapist and typically range from $80-$200 per session. Each therapist sets their own rates, which are clearly displayed on their profile. We also offer subscription plans ($29-$79/month) that include platform features and can reduce session costs. Some therapists accept insurance.

Do you accept insurance?

+
Some therapists on our platform accept insurance directly. You can filter therapists by insurance provider. For out-of-network benefits, we provide superbills that you can submit to your insurance company for reimbursement. Check with your insurance provider about telehealth coverage and reimbursement rates.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and PayPal. Payment is securely processed and charged at the time of booking or according to your subscription plan.

What do I need for a video session?

+
You'll need a device with a camera and microphone (computer, tablet, or smartphone), a stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps), and a private, quiet space. We recommend using Chrome, Safari, or Firefox browsers. Test your connection before your first session using the "Test Video" feature in your account settings.

What if I have technical issues during a session?

+
If you experience technical difficulties, try refreshing your browser, checking your internet connection, or switching to phone audio. Your therapist will have your phone number and can call you if video fails. Contact our 24/7 technical support at support@therapyconnect.com or via chat. Sessions interrupted by technical issues won't be charged.

Is my information secure and confidential?

+
Absolutely. TherapyConnect is HIPAA-compliant and uses bank-level encryption (AES-256) to protect your data. All communications with therapists are confidential and encrypted. We never sell your information. Therapists are bound by professional ethics and confidentiality laws. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Are sessions recorded?

+
No, sessions are not automatically recorded. Recording can only occur with explicit written consent from both you and your therapist. Unauthorized recording is prohibited and may be illegal. Session notes are kept by your therapist according to professional standards and are stored securely.

How do I join TherapyConnect as a therapist?

+
We're always looking for qualified mental health professionals! Visit our "For Therapists" page and click "Apply Now." You'll need to provide your license information, credentials, liability insurance, and complete a background check. Our team will review your application within 5-7 business days.
