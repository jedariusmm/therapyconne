1. Introduction

TherapyConnect ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mental health services platform.

Important: By using TherapyConnect, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that identifies you as an individual, including:

  • Full name, date of birth, and gender
  • Email address and phone number
  • Mailing address and billing information
  • Emergency contact information
  • Insurance information (if applicable)

2.2 Protected Health Information (PHI)

As a HIPAA-covered entity, we collect health information including:

  • Mental health history and current symptoms
  • Treatment notes and clinical assessments
  • Medication information
  • Session recordings (with your explicit consent)
  • Progress reports and treatment plans

2.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

  • IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and click patterns
  • Session dates and durations
  • Device identifiers and location data (with permission)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

3.1 Treatment & Care

  • Providing mental health services and therapy sessions
  • Creating and maintaining treatment plans
  • Coordinating care between healthcare providers (with consent)
  • Sending appointment reminders and follow-ups

3.2 Platform Operations

  • Managing your account and processing payments
  • Matching you with appropriate therapists
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Providing customer support

3.3 Legal & Compliance

  • Complying with legal obligations and regulations
  • Protecting against fraud and abuse
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Responding to legal requests and preventing harm

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security assessments and penetration testing
  • Data Minimization: We only collect what's necessary for treatment
  • Secure Infrastructure: HIPAA-compliant cloud hosting with redundancy
  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training for all staff
Important: While we use industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. HIPAA Compliance

TherapyConnect is a HIPAA-covered entity and complies with all applicable provisions of the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

5.1 Notice of Privacy Practices

In addition to this Privacy Policy, you will receive our Notice of Privacy Practices (NPP) which details how your Protected Health Information (PHI) may be used and disclosed for treatment, payment, and healthcare operations.

5.2 Business Associates

We only work with third-party service providers who sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) and agree to safeguard your PHI in compliance with HIPAA.

5.3 Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach involving your PHI, we will notify you as required by HIPAA breach notification rules, typically within 60 days of discovery.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

6.1 With Your Consent

  • With family members or caregivers you authorize
  • With other healthcare providers you specify
  • For purposes you explicitly approve

6.2 For Treatment

  • With your assigned therapist and clinical team
  • For care coordination with other providers (with consent)
  • In case of medical emergencies

6.3 Service Providers

  • Payment processors (for billing)
  • Cloud hosting services (HIPAA-compliant)
  • Analytics providers (aggregated, de-identified data only)

6.4 Legal Requirements

  • To comply with court orders or legal processes
  • To prevent imminent harm to you or others
  • To report suspected abuse or neglect
  • As required by law enforcement or regulatory agencies

7. Your Rights

Under HIPAA and applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

7.1 Access Your Information

You have the right to request a copy of your health records and personal information.

7.2 Request Corrections

You can request amendments to incorrect or incomplete information.

7.3 Request Restrictions

You can ask us to limit how we use or disclose your information for treatment, payment, or operations.

7.4 Confidential Communications

You can request that we communicate with you in a specific way or at a specific location.

7.5 Accounting of Disclosures

You can request a list of certain disclosures we've made of your information.

7.6 Revoke Authorization

You can revoke any authorization you've given us, except to the extent we've already acted on it.

7.7 Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your account, subject to legal retention requirements.

To exercise these rights: Contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@therapyconnect.com or use the Privacy Settings in your account.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

8.1 Essential Cookies

Required for basic platform functionality, authentication, and security.

8.2 Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how users interact with our platform (anonymized).

8.3 Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

TherapyConnect provides services to minors (under 18) with parental consent. For users under 13, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Parents/guardians can review, modify, or delete their child's information at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on our platform. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
